📄 Article to PDF Extractor

Convert articles into clean PDFs — including content behind paywalls you're licensed for

How this handles paywalls: A browser-only tool can't log in to WSJ, NYT, JSTOR, etc. on its own, and you've said you don't want to give it credentials — good call. Instead, this tool piggybacks on your already-logged-in browser. Use the Bookmarklet tab for paywalled articles (one click on the article page, paste here), the Saved HTML tab for pages you've saved from your browser, or the URL Fetch tab for public pages.
